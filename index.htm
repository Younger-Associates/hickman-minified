About Hickman County, TN

Hickman County is a great place to live if you like fresh air, trees, fresh spring water, a rich river system, scenic views, friendly people, small farms, and small town life. We're blessed.

Hickman County, Tennessee is more than our great weather and beautiful natural environment; we combine a skilled workforce, strategic location in the Southeast US, and competitive business costs to fuel our business ecosystem. If you invest with us, you'll have a part in helping a community develop into a vibrant center. You'll help us bring some of the best of our workers home from jobs outside of the county. Our young people will seek employment, education, and leadership in this region because this is a great place to be. This is why we are a great location for the future.
